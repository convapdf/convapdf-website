Meet the Team Behind the Revolution

Our Mission is to Make
Printing Instant & Effortless.

ConvaPDF started with a single, clear vision: to eliminate bulky, legacy desktop billing terminals and provide business owners, logistics teams, and creatives with a truly portable thermal printing suite.

The Values We Print By

We hold ourselves to high technical and ecological standards to keep your workflows efficient.

Eco-Friendly Legacy

By engineering high-performance thermal mechanics, we save thousands of cartridges, ribbons, and hazardous chemical toners from landfills annually.

Mobile-First Ecosystem

We prioritize cloud connectivity and instant app actions over hardware buttons, giving you total creative customization from any smart device.

Uncompromising Quality

We manufacture hardware components with robust, shock-resistant frames and source long-lasting, smudge-proof thermal paper rolls.

How We Got Here

Phase 1: The Spark

Addressing Desktop Pain Points

The legacy billing space was broken. High paper waste, massive ink costs, and stationary devices held on-the-go vendors hostage. We set out to make printing wireless and dynamic.

Phase 2: R&D

Designing Pocket Hardware

We paired standard Bluetooth 5.2 architecture with high-efficiency 200dpi thermal micro-controllers to form our first ultra-compact prototype, paving the way for silent billing.

Phase 3: Ecosystem Launch

SaaS Meets Thermal Hardware

With the release of the ConvaPDF companion app, users could transform PDFs and custom designs directly into print-optimized black-and-white formats within milliseconds.
